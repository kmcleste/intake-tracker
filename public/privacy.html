Food & Symptom Journal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Food & Symptom Journal ("the app") is a personal health tracking tool. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights over it.

Data We Collect

The app collects only the data you choose to enter:

  • Account information — your email address, used solely for authentication.
  • Journal entries — foods, symptoms, wellbeing scores, notes, tags, and optional photos you log.
  • Care team links — email addresses of caregivers you invite, and notes they add to your entries.
  • Usage audit log — a record of actions (entry created, updated, deleted) for your own review.

We do not collect device identifiers, location data, browsing history, or advertising identifiers.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To display and sync your journal entries across devices.
  • To enable caregiver sharing when you choose to invite someone.
  • To generate trends and analytics shown within the app.

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Row-level security policies ensure each user can only access their own data.

Photos

Photos you attach to journal entries are stored in Supabase Storage and are only accessible to you (and caregivers you have explicitly invited). Photos are not used for any purpose other than display within the app.

Caregiver Access

If you invite a caregiver, they can view your journal entries and add private observations. You control which of their notes are visible to you. You can remove a caregiver at any time from within the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete individual entries at any time (with a 30-day recovery window). To permanently delete your account and all associated data, use the "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings panel. Upon deletion, all your entries, notes, photos, and account information are permanently removed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
